How to Use the Virtual Fitting Room

  1. 1

    Upload your images

    First, upload a full-body photo of yourself or a model. Then, upload a clear picture of the clothing item you want to try on.

  2. 2

    Enter your prompt

    Write a simple text prompt telling the AI what to do. For example, "Put this shirt on the person in the photo."

  3. 3

    Generate your virtual try-on

    Our AI will process your images and prompt to create a new image of the person wearing the selected clothes.

    Your virtual try-on image is ready. Download the high-resolution PNG file to share or use for your online store.
