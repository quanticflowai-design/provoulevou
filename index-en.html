The Process

Three steps to materialization.

Simple for the merchant, magical for the customer.

Click "Try It On Me"

The button is integrated into your product photos, allowing the customer to start the experience instantly.

Step 02

Enter Your Details

The customer enters height, weight, and email so our AI can analyze body type, dress them in the selected item, and recommend the ideal size with precision.

Materialize the Result

Our AI does the heavy lifting and delivers a realistic image with perfect fit, and even indicates the ideal size for the chosen item.
